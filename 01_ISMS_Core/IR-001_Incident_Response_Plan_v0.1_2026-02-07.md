## 1. Purpose
Provide a consistent process to detect, triage, contain, eradicate, recover, and learn from security incidents.

## 2. Severity Levels
- Sev1: major customer impact / sensitive data risk
- Sev2: limited impact / contained quickly
- Sev3: low impact / informational

## 3. Workflow
1) Detect and log event  
2) Triage within 1 business day  
3) Contain and preserve evidence  
4) Eradicate root cause  
5) Recover services/data  
6) Post-incident review and CAPA where applicable
