## 1. Purpose
This policy establishes CloudFin Analytics’ commitment to protect information assets and to operate an Information Security Management System (ISMS) that supports business objectives, reduces information security risks, and continuously improves security performance.

## 2. Scope
This policy applies to all CloudFin Analytics personnel (employees and contractors) and to all information assets and processes within the ISMS scope defined in ISMS-001 (ISMS Scope), including the CloudFin SaaS platform, supporting corporate tools, and relevant suppliers.

## 3. Policy Statement
CloudFin Analytics is committed to:
- Protecting the **confidentiality, integrity, and availability** of information processed, stored, or transmitted within the ISMS scope.
- Implementing and maintaining an ISMS aligned with ISO/IEC 27001 requirements and relevant Annex A controls, based on risk.
- Meeting applicable legal, regulatory, and contractual information security obligations relevant to CloudFin’s services and customer commitments.
- Ensuring that information security responsibilities are understood and followed by all personnel through appropriate awareness and training.
- Maintaining evidence-based security controls, records, and monitoring to support assurance, accountability, and continual improvement.

## 4. Roles and Responsibilities (High-Level)
- **Top Management:** Provides direction, resources, and oversight of the ISMS; reviews ISMS performance and objectives.
- **Security/GRC (ISMS Owner):** Maintains ISMS documentation, risk management, SoA, internal audit program, and corrective actions.
- **Engineering / Platform/DevOps:** Implements and operates technical controls, change management, secure development practices, monitoring, and backups.
- **Customer Support:** Handles customer communications and support records in line with data handling and access requirements.
- **All Personnel:** Follow security policies and procedures, report security events, and protect company and customer data.

## 5. Risk Management Commitment
CloudFin Analytics will identify, assess, and treat information security risks using the methodology defined in ISMS-003. Risk treatment decisions will be recorded in the Risk Register (ISMS-004) and controls will be recorded in the Statement of Applicability (ISMS-005) with traceable evidence.

## 6. Objectives
Information security objectives will be established, tracked, and reviewed. Objectives will be measurable where practical (e.g., vulnerability remediation timelines, access review completion, backup/restore testing cadence, incident response performance).

## 7. Compliance and Enforcement
Violations of this policy may result in corrective action, up to and including removal of access or disciplinary action, depending on severity. Supplier compliance will be addressed through contractual and operational oversight.

## 8. Continual Improvement
CloudFin Analytics will continually improve the ISMS by:
- Monitoring performance and security events
- Conducting internal audits and management reviews
- Tracking corrective actions (CAPA) to closure
- Updating controls and documentation based on risk, changes, and lessons learned

## 9. Review and Approval
This policy is reviewed at least annually and upon significant change to the business, technology, or threat landscape.
