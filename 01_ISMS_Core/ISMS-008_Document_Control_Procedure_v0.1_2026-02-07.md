## 1. Purpose
Ensure ISMS documented information is approved, versioned, protected from unintended changes, and retrievable for audits.

## 2. Scope
Applies to all ISMS documents/records in this repository (policies, procedures, registers, audits, records, evidence index).

## 3. Rules
- Every document has: Document ID, Title, Owner, Version, Status, Date.
- Controlled updates occur via PR workflow.
- Each key document includes an Approval line (simulated acceptable) and a Change History table.

## 4. Approval and Review
- Draft documents are reviewed and approved by Management (Simulated) or ISMS Owner.
- Review frequency: at least annually or after major change.

## 5. Change History (minimum)
Each key document maintains a change history table:
| Version | Date | Change Summary | Author | Approver |
|---|---|---|---|---|
